Cadence · Privacy Policy

Your data stays with you.

Last updated: 29 May 2026

The short version

Cadence is a local-first habit-sequence planner. Your goals, sub-habits, energy profile, and progress live on your device by default. Optional Google sign-in backs up your goals and completion history to the cloud so they sync across your devices — it is off until you choose to sign in. There is no advertising. There is no third-party analytics SDK in this version of the app. We never sell your data.

What is stored, and where

  • On your device: all goal titles, sub-habit names, completion state, timeline blocks, energy profile, onboarding flag, and your consent record. This is kept in your device’s local storage (browser localStorage on the web; the WebView’s local storage on iOS and Android).
  • In the cloud, only if you sign in: your account identifier and email (from Google), your goals, and your completion history — stored in our Supabase database and isolated to your account. This powers cross-device sync and your rhythm insights.
  • What we never collect: phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, files, advertising ID, or device identifiers.

Optional AI sequence generation

When you type a goal into “New goal → sequence” and press Generate, Cadence breaks it into a suggested micro-sequence. In the current released app this is produced on your device — your goal text does not leave your device for this feature. If a future version routes sequence generation to an external AI service, that request would be sent over HTTPS and we will update this notice before enabling it.

Optional sync

Signing in with Google enables optional cross-device sync. Your data is transmitted over HTTPS, stored encrypted at rest by our database provider (Supabase), and isolated to your account with row-level security. Sync is off until you sign in, and you can withdraw consent and erase everything at any time from Settings. Note: data is encrypted in transit and at rest, but not end-to-end — the database provider can technically access stored rows.

Permissions Cadence requests

  • Internet — used only for the optional Google sign-in and cloud sync. Fonts are bundled inside the app, and sequence generation runs on-device. Cadence works fully offline if you never sign in.

Cadence does not request the camera, microphone, contacts, location, calendar, notifications (yet), files, or any other sensitive permission.

Your rights

Because all data is on-device, you control it directly:

  • Delete everything: Settings → “Erase all my data” deletes your local and cloud data, or uninstall the app / clear app data in your OS settings.
  • Export everything: Settings → “Download my data” exports everything we hold as JSON.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how data is handled — for example, if we ship sync — we will update this page and bump the “last updated” date. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Your rights under India’s DPDP Act, 2023

For users in India, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (Act No. 22 of 2023) applies. Under it you are a Data Principal and Cadence is the Data Fiduciary. We process your personal data only on the legal basis of your consent, which you give when you sign in, and for the specific purpose of providing and syncing your planner.

  • Right to notice & purpose: we collect only your account identifier (from Google sign-in), your goals, sub-habits, and completion history — solely to run the app and sync across your devices. We do not sell data or use it for advertising.
  • Right to access: Settings → “Download my data” exports everything we hold as JSON.
  • Right to correction & erasure: edit any data in-app; Settings → “Erase all my data” permanently deletes your local data, your cloud data, and your account itself (including your email). See how to delete your account & data.
  • Right to withdraw consent: sign out and erase your data at any time — as easily as you gave consent. Withdrawal does not affect processing already done.
  • Right to grievance redressal: contact our grievance point below; we respond within the timelines prescribed under the Act.
  • Right to nominate: you may nominate another person to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity — email us to register a nominee.

Children

Under the DPDP Act a “child” is anyone under 18 years. Cadence is intended only for users who are 18 years of age or older. At sign-in you must confirm you are 18+. We do not knowingly collect or process the personal data of anyone under 18; if you are under 18, please do not sign in or use the cloud features. We do not undertake tracking, behavioural monitoring, or targeted advertising directed at children. If we learn that we have processed a minor’s data, we will delete it.

Data retention

We retain your data only while your account is active and the purpose (running your planner) is served. When you erase your data or withdraw consent, your cloud rows are deleted immediately and local data is cleared from your device.

Security & breach

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and isolated per user with database row-level security. In the event of a personal-data breach, we will notify the Data Protection Board of India and affected users as required by the Act.
