Pacely · Privacy Policy

Your data stays with you.

Last updated: 4 July 2026

The short version

Pacely is a local-first habit-sequence planner. Your goals, sub-habits, energy profile, and progress live on your device by default. Optional Google sign-in backs up your goals and completion history to the cloud so they sync across your devices — it is off until you choose to sign in. Optional voice planning sends your spoken description of your day to our private server to build a plan — the audio is processed transiently and never stored. If you choose to add a plan to your calendar, Pacely writes those events into your device’s calendar with your permission; it never reads your existing calendar. There is no advertising. There is no third-party analytics SDK in this version of the app. We never sell your data.

What is stored, and where

  • On your device: all goal titles, sub-habit names, completion state, timeline blocks, energy profile, onboarding flag, and your consent record. This is kept in your device’s local storage (browser localStorage on the web; the WebView’s local storage on iOS and Android).
  • In the cloud, only if you sign in: your account identifier, name, email and phone number, plus your goals and completion history — stored in our Supabase database and isolated to your account. This powers cross-device sync and your rhythm insights.
  • Never stored anywhere: voice recordings. Audio from voice planning is processed in memory to produce your transcript and plan, then discarded — it is not written to disk, logged, or used to train any model.
  • In your device’s calendar, only if you ask: when you use “add to my calendar”, Pacely writes your plan’s steps as calendar events (with reminder alarms) into the calendar you choose. These live in your calendar — not on our servers — and Pacely does not read them back or send them anywhere. If your calendar itself syncs to a provider (e.g. Google Calendar), those events go wherever that calendar goes, under that provider’s policy.
  • What we never collect: precise location, contacts, photos, files, advertising ID, device identifiers, or the contents of your existing calendar.

Optional AI sequence generation

When you type a goal into “New goal → sequence” and press Generate, Pacely breaks it into a suggested micro-sequence. In the current released app this is produced on your device — your goal text does not leave your device for this feature. If a future version routes sequence generation to an external AI service, that request would be sent over HTTPS and we will update this notice before enabling it.

Optional voice planning

The “Voice plan” feature on the Today screen lets you describe your day out loud and get a plan back. It is entirely user-initiated: recording starts only when you open the voice sheet, and stops when you tap “Done talking”. Your audio is sent over HTTPS, through our own gateway (available only to signed-in users), to a private inference server we operate running open-source speech-recognition and language models. It is not sent to any third-party AI service, and neither the audio nor the transcript is used to train any model. The audio is processed in memory to produce a transcript and a suggested plan, both returned to your device, and the recording is then discarded — we do not store voice recordings. The plan you accept is saved like any other goal you create. Our infrastructure providers (who run the servers, but do not use the data for their own purposes) are Supabase and Modal. If you never use voice planning, the microphone is never accessed.

Optional sync

Signing in with Google enables optional cross-device sync. Your data is transmitted over HTTPS, stored encrypted at rest by our database provider (Supabase), and isolated to your account with row-level security. Sync is off until you sign in, and you can withdraw consent and erase everything at any time from Settings. Note: data is encrypted in transit and at rest, but not end-to-end — the database provider can technically access stored rows.

Permissions Pacely requests

  • Internet — used only for the optional Google sign-in and cloud sync. Fonts are bundled inside the app, and sequence generation runs on-device. Pacely works fully offline if you never sign in.
  • Notifications — optional. If you turn on the daily reminder, Pacely schedules a local notification on your device at the time you choose. It is off by default, the reminder is generated entirely on-device, and no data is sent for it.
  • Microphone — optional, only for voice planning. The OS asks for your permission the first time you use the feature; recording happens only while the voice sheet is open, and the audio is processed transiently on our private server and never stored (see “Optional voice planning” above). Deny the permission and everything else in Pacely still works.
  • Calendar (write-only) — optional, only when you tap “add to my calendar” on a plan. The OS asks for your permission the first time; Pacely then adds your plan’s steps as events with reminders to the calendar you choose. It requests write access to add events — it does not read your existing calendar, and it never sends calendar data to our servers. Deny the permission and Pacely falls back to handing you a calendar file to import yourself; everything else still works.

Pacely does not request the camera, contacts, location, photos, files, or any other sensitive permission, and it never reads your existing calendar — the calendar permission is used only to add the events you asked for.

Your rights

Because all data is on-device, you control it directly:

  • Delete everything: Settings → “Erase all my data” deletes your local and cloud data, or uninstall the app / clear app data in your OS settings.
  • Export everything: Settings → “Download my data” exports everything we hold as JSON.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how data is handled — for example, if we ship sync — we will update this page and bump the “last updated” date. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Your rights under India’s DPDP Act, 2023

For users in India, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (Act No. 22 of 2023) applies. Under it you are a Data Principal and Pacely is the Data Fiduciary. We process your personal data only on the legal basis of your consent, which you give when you sign in, and for the specific purpose of providing and syncing your planner.

  • Right to notice & purpose: we collect your account identifier, name, email and phone number (name and email from Google sign-in; phone from the profile step), plus your goals, sub-habits, and completion history — solely to run the app, contact you about your account, and sync across your devices. If you use voice planning, your voice audio is processed transiently for the sole purpose of generating your plan and is not retained. If you use “add to my calendar”, we write your chosen plan steps to your device calendar with your permission — this data stays in your calendar and is not collected by us. We do not sell data or use it for advertising.
  • Right to access: Settings → “Download my data” exports everything we hold as JSON.
  • Right to correction & erasure: edit any data in-app; Settings → “Erase all my data” permanently deletes your local data, your cloud data, and your account itself (including your email). See how to delete your account & data.
  • Right to withdraw consent: sign out and erase your data at any time — as easily as you gave consent. Withdrawal does not affect processing already done.
  • Right to grievance redressal: contact our grievance point below; we respond within the timelines prescribed under the Act.
  • Right to nominate: you may nominate another person to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity — email us to register a nominee.

Children

Under the DPDP Act a “child” is anyone under 18 years. Pacely is intended only for users who are 18 years of age or older. At sign-in you must confirm you are 18+. We do not knowingly collect or process the personal data of anyone under 18; if you are under 18, please do not sign in or use the cloud features. We do not undertake tracking, behavioural monitoring, or targeted advertising directed at children. If we learn that we have processed a minor’s data, we will delete it.

Data retention

We retain your data only while your account is active and the purpose (running your planner) is served. When you erase your data or withdraw consent, your cloud rows are deleted immediately and local data is cleared from your device.

Security & breach

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and isolated per user with database row-level security. In the event of a personal-data breach, we will notify the Data Protection Board of India and affected users as required by the Act.
