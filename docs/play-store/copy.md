## Full description (≤ 4000 chars)
```
Cadence is a habit planner that does the planning for you.

You give it a goal — "finish the design review", "run a 5K by August", "read one chapter a day" — and it breaks it into a sequence of tiny, time-estimated micro-steps you can actually start. The first step is always under five minutes. The hardest step lands when your energy is highest. A short rest goes between the heavy ones.

This is not another streak app. Cadence is built for people whose attention is a real variable, not a moral failing — neurodivergent, burned-out, recovering, or just done with productivity guilt.

WHAT'S DIFFERENT

· Goal → sequence in one tap. Type your goal, pick your energy and the hours you have today, and Cadence proposes a five-to-seven-step plan with realistic minute estimates and a one-line "why" beside each step.

· Daily, weekly, monthly, or one-off. Each goal carries its own rhythm. Filter by cadence so today's view stays calm. Recurring goals reset themselves at the start of each period — no manual reset, no shame.

· Many sub-habits, your way. Add as many sub-habits as you want under a goal. Rename inline, change minutes, reorder with arrows, check them off. Build a whole routine by hand if the AI guessed wrong.

· Adapts when life happens. Missed a day? Slept badly? Cadence rebalances the rest of your sequence — never punishes a missed day with a broken streak.

· Visual timeline blocks, not endless lists. Today is a stacked schedule, not a checkbox graveyard. See the shape of your day at a glance.

· Effort bloom, not red streaks. Progress is shown as light, not as red marks for missed days. Done is celebrated. Not-done is just neutral.

· Local-first by default. Your goals, sub-habits, and progress live on your device. No account required. No advertising. No third-party trackers in this version of the app.

· Offline ready. The AI breakdown needs a one-shot network call, but the rest of the app — including a built-in starter sequence — works without a connection.

THE PIECES

· Today — your sequenced day as time-blocks. Quick chips for voice capture, life-happened replans, and the template library.

· Goals — every active sequence in one place. Tap any card to drill into the goal inline, edit its sub-habits, and swipe through neighbouring goals with the ‹ › chips at the top.

· Insights — patterns, not pressure. Which hours work for you? Which goals stall? Suggestions, never demands.

· You — energy profile, replay onboarding, opt out of features.

WHO IT'S FOR

· Anyone whose attention is variable: ADHD, autism, anxiety, depression, long-COVID brain fog, post-burnout recovery.
· People burned out by streak apps that turn one missed day into a relapse spiral.
· Designers, writers, founders, and students whose work has lumpy demand and ambiguous next steps.
· Anyone who is tired of motivation-shaped advice.

PRIVACY

All data — every goal name, every sub-habit, every completion — lives only on your device. The optional AI sequence generation sends a single goal-text request to a language model and we do not log it. Full policy at the link in this listing.

Cadence is built and maintained by an independent developer. Source code is open at github.com/acash17/habit-tracker.
```
