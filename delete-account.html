Cadence · Account & data deletion

Delete your account and data.

Your right to erasure · DPDP Act, 2023

Cadence lets you delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do it yourself inside the app, or request it from us — both remove the same data.

Option 1 — Delete it yourself in the app (instant)

  1. Open Cadence and go to Settings → “You”.
  2. Under “Your data & privacy”, tap “Erase all my data” → Confirm.
  3. This immediately deletes your account and all its data — goals, completion history, profile, and consent record, both on your device and in our cloud — and signs you out.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Email grievance@vinkashis.com from the address you signed in with, with the subject “Delete my Cadence account and data.”

We will verify your request and erase your account and all associated data within 30 days, and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Your account itself, including your account identifier and email (from Google sign-in)
  • Your goals and sub-habits
  • Your completion history
  • Your profile and settings
  • Your consent records

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. We do not retain backups of deleted personal data beyond the period required to complete the deletion.

Questions

Grievance / Data Protection contact: grievance@vinkashis.com. See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. You may also complain to the Data Protection Board of India.
